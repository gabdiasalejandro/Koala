main:: 1 Teaching Sequence
Organizes the visual explanation of a topic from concept to reinforcement.

hl:: starts with -> 1.1 Core Idea
Introduces the main idea with the strongest emphasis.

note:: expands into -> 1.1.1 Supporting Context
Adds clarifying information that helps the reader follow the sequence.

warn:: prevents -> 1.1.2 Common Confusion
Marks the misconception or caution that should not be ignored.

soft:: closes with -> 1.1.3 Secondary Detail
Adds a lower-priority reminder that still helps complete the explanation.

1.2 Practice Layer
Turns the explanation into something easier to recall and teach.

hl:: reinforces -> 1.2.1 Key Example
Connects the explanation to a concrete didactic case.

note:: summarizes -> 1.2.2 Short Recap
Ends the branch with a compact synthesis.
